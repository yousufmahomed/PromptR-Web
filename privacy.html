PromptR Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Introduction

PromptR ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

PromptR accesses your device's camera and microphone solely for the purpose of recording videos. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All videos are stored locally on your device.

Camera and Microphone Access

PromptR requires access to your device's camera and microphone to record videos. This access is used exclusively for video recording functionality within the app. We do not access your camera or microphone for any other purpose.

Data Storage

All videos recorded using PromptR are stored locally on your device. We do not upload, transmit, or store your videos on any external servers or cloud services.

Third-Party Services

PromptR may use third-party services for in-app purchases (Google Play Billing). These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

In-App Purchases

PromptR offers in-app purchases processed through Google Play. Payment information is handled by Google and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

PromptR is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
